Groupingplugin

A Minecraft-Plugin to create and handle private Groups

Welcome to the Groupingplugin Page

The Groupingplugin-Plugin allows Players on the server to create and manage Groups. Inside groups XP will be shared and in Combination with my other plugins you can recieve and solve Quests together and even play with different classes to enjoy Buffs for the whole group.

Easy to use

Simply create a group and invite a friend to join you and you're ready to go

MMORPG-like Adventure

In Combination with my other Plugins you can expirience a whole new adventure in Minecraft to play and quest with your friends.
